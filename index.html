Welcome to the Vision and Learning Group

The Vision and Learning Group (VLG) was established within the Computer Software Institute at Weifang University of Science and Technology in June, 2020. The VLG focuses on the research field in digital image processing, computer vision, machine learning and deep learning. Our current research interests include the following topics but not limited to:

  • Object Detection
  • Image Segmentation
  • 3D Point Cloud Processing
