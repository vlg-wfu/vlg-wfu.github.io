Recruitment and Opportunities

我们组现招本科生参与科研项目。 招收对象：计算机，电子工程，电气自动化等相关专业。
最低要求(掌握下列每项技能，每项至少熟悉一种)：

  • 编程语言： C/C++; Java; Python; Matlab; Julia; R; JavaScript等
  • 数学基础: 线性代数，微积分，概率与统计
  • 专业基础： 数字信号处理

    • 以下所列为可选择项(掌握以下技能者优先)：

  • 相关课程: 数字图像处理，计算机视觉
  • 人工智能: 机器学习，深度学习，启发式算法

    • 联系方式：liuguoxu@wfust.edu.cn

    We are looking for undergraduate students!

    Criteria: self-motivated, hardworking….
    Background: computer science; electronics engineering; electrical automation….
    The above criteria and background are the basic requirments. Moreover, ensure that you have covered all the listed skills (at least one item for each type of skills).

  • Programming languages: C/C++; Java; Python; Matlab; Julia; R; JavaScript; etc
  • Mathematic basics: linear algebra; calculus; probability and statistics
  • Major basics: digital signal processing

    • The following criteria are optional requirments：

  • Relavant courses: digital image processing; computer vision
  • Artificial intelligence: machine learning; deep learning; metaheuristics

    • Email: liuguoxu@wfust.edu.cn
